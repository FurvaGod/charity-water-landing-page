The Power to End This Crisis Lives Within Us

While we debate what to stream tonight, 703 million people will spend tomorrow walking for water that threatens their very survival.

Picture this: A young girl misses school—not because she doesn't want to learn, but because she must walk 6 hours to fetch water for her family. A mother loses her baby to waterborne disease that could have been prevented with clean water.

Here's what gives us hope: this crisis has a solution. For the cost of a few coffee runs, we can provide someone with clean water for life. This is our generation's moonshot—and it's completely achievable.
